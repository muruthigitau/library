1.
Adding a Book
- Go to the Books list.
- Click on New Book.
- Fill in the details and save.
2.
Registering a Member
- Go to the Members list.
- Click on New Member.
- Enter the member details and save.
3.
Issuing a Book
- Go to the Transactions list.
- Click New Transaction.
- Choose Issue as the type, select the books and member, and save.
4.
Returning a Book
- Go to the Transactions list.
- Find the relevant transaction.
- Click Return Book. The system will calculate any fees and update the book's availability.
5.
Making a Payment
- After returning the book, go to the Transactions list.
- Select the transaction and click Make Payment.
- Enter the payment amount, then save to complete the payment.
6.
Reserving a Book
- Go to the Books list, search for the book.
- Click Reserve Book, select the member, and save.
7.
Managing Settings
- Go to My Settings.
- Update debt limits, late fees, etc.
